August Grey Released By WWE

Publish date: 2024-01-18

has been released by WWE.

According to PWInsider.com, August Grey was released by the company on Friday afternoon.

Known on the independent scene as Anthony Greene (aka Retro AG), August Grey was a regular on WWE 205 Live and was even set to have a match on tonight’s episode of 205 against Asher Hale. Grey leaned into his release on social media by Tweeting the following:

“*BREAKING NEWS* Tonight’s WWE 205 Live Main Event has just been turned into a LOSER LEAVES TOWN MATCH! Don’t miss it!!!”

*BREAKING NEWS*

Tonight’s @WWE205Live Main Event has just been turned into a LOSER LEAVES TOWN MATCH!

Don’t miss it!!! pic.twitter.com/SmApTs3ugu

— Anthony Greene アンソニー・グリーン (@alternative_ag) June 25, 2021

Greene was a relatively recent signee of WWE as he inked a developmental deal with the company last August. Greene spend a great deal of his independent wrestling time with Northeast Wrestling, but was a very popular talent to use on the independent scene overall before his signing with WWE.
