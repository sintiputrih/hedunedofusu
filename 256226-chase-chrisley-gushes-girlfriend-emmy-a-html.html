Chase Chrisley Gushes over Girlfriend Emmy under a Sweet Video He Posted of Her

“Chrisley Knows Best” star Chase Chrisley took to social media to gush over his girlfriend, Emmy Medders, after posting a snippet video of her on his account.

Chase Chrisley shared how "lucky" he felt in a recent upload to social media featuring his girlfriend, Emmy Medders.

Alongside a cute clip of Medders admiring herself, Chrisley wrote: “Hubba hubba, I'm a lucky guy @emmymedders." Along with the post, he shared Medders’ cute clip while admiring herself.

CHASE GUSHES OVER HIS LOVER

Chase's fans quickly pointed out how beautiful she is, while another said they believed it was his sister. "I thought it was Savannah for a second," said one user.

Another commented on her attractive features and compared Medders to a doll. "She looks like a real-life baby doll...bless her heart. Beautiful young lady. Glad you found each other."

While sharing how good they look together, a third added that Chase should hurry up and marry her. “She’s beautiful, congratulations Chase. Hurry up n put a ring on it!!!” said the user.

Medders is indeed a beauty. On the video, she appeared to be shaking her head sideways, checking herself out. She put on glamorous makeup and opted for a glowy look with a nude lip.

She wore a black nylon top and accessorized with hoop earrings that have pearl details. Medders also wore a gold necklace and wore her blonde hair down.

BEING CALLED A DIVA

According to TV Show Ace, last month she called her boyfriend a 'diva’ while massaging his face. Medders reportedly posted a video of Chase on her Insta Stories.

On the clip, she added some cucumber slice stickers to his eyes. She then went to the caption and wrote that he is a diva. Following her statement, Chase reportedly opened his eyes and smiled at the camera.

CHASE’S BUSINESS VENTURE

Not only is Chase smitten with his girlfriend, but he makes sure he does what he is passionate about too. According to Us Magazine, the 24-year-old launched his line of candles, Chase Chrisley Collection, back in November 2020.

Last month, the outlet reported that they quickly sold out at the time but are now back in stock as of February 25. Speaking on his new business venture, Chase revealed that they have always been a passion of his and that he gets it from his mom.

He further explained that when growing up, his mom always had candles lit which controlled the room's mood. Chase also shared he uses candles on a daily and wants his fans to experience them too.

KYLE CHRISLEY IS ENGAGED

As Chase continues to embrace his relationship and doing what he loves the most, his brother is already set to get married. According to The Sun, Kyle is engaged to girlfriend Ashleigh Nelson.

The exciting news came after he reconciled with his famous father and family. The 29-year-old reportedly asked for Nelson’s hand in marriage on Valentine’s Day.

The pair have been dating for over a year and kept their relationship away from the limelight. However, it appears Kyle could not keep the news to himself any longer and announced his engagement on Instagram.
