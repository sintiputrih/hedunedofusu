Andrea Jordan - InStyle

Publish date: 2024-10-06

Title: Contributing Writer

Education: Temple University

Location: New Jersey

Expertise: Beauty, Home, Food, Family

  • Andrea Jordan is a freelance writer who loves to write about all things beauty, home, food, and family.
  • Born and raised in a suburban area of Ohio, Andrea took on her big-city dreams and moved to the New York City area after graduating college. She currently lives in New Jersey with her husband. 
  • When she’s not writing, you can find her curled up on the couch reading a book or in the kitchen whipping up a new recipe.

Experience

Andrea began her career writing beauty content at Essence Magazine. She is now an experienced writer and editor, with her work appearing in many publications including Real Simple, InStyle, Allure, Glamour, TODAY, Better Homes & Gardens, StyleCaster, and PopSugar.

Education

Andrea holds a BA from Temple University in Magazine Journalism and Public Relations.
