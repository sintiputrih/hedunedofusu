Art Briles’ Background

Arthur Ray Briles, born on December 3, 1955, is a former American football player and current football coach at Baylor University, a position he has held since 2008. He previously served as the head coach at the University of Houston from 2002 to 2007. Briles is also known for his book “Beating Goliath: My Story of Football and Faith” (2014).
