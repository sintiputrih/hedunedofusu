Ashley Olsen Is Dating Moneyball Director Bennett Miller

Publish date: 2024-05-07

Ashley Olsen‘s new movie man!

Following her recent split with businessman David Schulte, the 27-year-old fashion designer is now dating Moneyball director Bennett Miller, multiple sources tell Us Weekly. See more photos of her here.

The new couple supported one another at the star-studded funeral of Oscar winner Philip Seymour Hoffman in NYC on Friday, Feb. 7. Miller, 47, was a close friend of Hoffman’s, and also directed the 2005 film Capote, which earned the late actor his only Oscar. 

“They were introduced and have started dating,” a source explains to Us, adding that Miller flew from L.A. to attend Hoffman’s funeral. Ashley spent time in Los Angeles with her new man back in January, where he’s been editing his latest film, Foxcatcher. 

Miller and Olsen were also spotting dining together at Dan Tana’s in West Hollywood on Jan. 24.
