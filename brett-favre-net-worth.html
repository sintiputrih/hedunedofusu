Brett Lorenzo Favre Wiki Biography

Brett Favre, an American Football player, was born on 10 October 1969, in Gulfport, Mississippi. He played quarterback for the Green Bay Packers in the NFL. He also played for the Atlanta Falcons, New York Jets, and Minnesota Vikings. Additionally, he pursued acting, coaching, and analysis.

Brett Favre’s estimated net worth, as of mid-2017, is over $110 million. His income substantially grew from 2000 onwards. His salary increased from $450,000 with a $3 million bonus in 2000 to $12 million from the New York Jets in 2008, and eventually to a $25 million contract in 2009 and a $12 million deal with the Vikings.

Favre’s passion for football began at Hancock North Central High School, although he initially focused on baseball. He continued his studies at the University of Southern Mississippi on a football scholarship and set various university records during his college career.

Favre’s NFL career was remarkable, particularly during his 15 years with the Green Bay Packers. Referred to as “The Gunslinger,” he achieved personal milestones and set numerous league records.

After retiring from professional football, Favre worked as an analyst, coached high school football, and made cameo appearances in films. He also owns Brett Favre’s Steakhouse in Green Bay.

In his personal life, Favre has been married to Deanna since 1996, and they have two daughters. He is also a philanthropist, having founded the Brett Favre Fourward Foundation in 1996.
