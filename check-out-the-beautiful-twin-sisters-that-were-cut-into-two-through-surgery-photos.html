Check Out The Beautiful Twin Sisters That Were Cut Into Two Through Surgery (PHOTOS)

Publish date: 2024-02-20

Kendra and Maliyah Herrin are twins from Salt Lake, Utah, who underwent surgery to separate them after they were born conjoined with two legs.

Doctors expressed fears they were not going to last more than 24 hours because they had only one kidney when they were born.

Reports revealed that they lived as conjoined twins for a long time until they underwent a medical procedure to detach them but Kendra was alright because she was the one with the kidney while Maliyah was on dialysis for some time.

They mother reportedly gave one of her kidneys to Maliyah which has taken her out of dialysis and after their operation, they have one leg each.

Today, Kendra and Maliyah are 18 years old and they are a living proof of God’s miraculous and unfathomable power.
