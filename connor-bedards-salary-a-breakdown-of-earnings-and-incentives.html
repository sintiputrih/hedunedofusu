Connor Bedard's Salary: A Breakdown of Earnings and Incentives

Publish date: 2024-06-17

Connor Bedard, the talented rookie for the Chicago Blackhawks, has recently signed a lucrative three-year contract. This article will provide a comprehensive breakdown of Bedard's salary, including base salary, signing bonus, cap hit, and potential incentives. Let's delve into the details of his earnings and understand the financial aspects of his contract.

The Three-Year Contract: Terms and Guarantees

Connor Bedard's contract with the Chicago Blackhawks spans three years and is valued at an impressive $13,350,000. This contract ensures that Bedard receives the full amount, guaranteeing financial security for the talented young player.

Base Salary and Signing Bonus

For the 2023-24 season, Bedard will receive a base salary of $855,000. Additionally, he will receive a signing bonus of $95,000. These two components make up Bedard's salary for the season, totaling $950,000.

Cap Hit and its Significance

The cap hit refers to the amount of money that counts against a team's salary cap. For the 2023-24 season, Bedard's cap hit is $950,000. This figure is crucial for teams, as it affects their ability to manage their roster and sign other players within the limitations of the salary cap.

Restricted Free Agency

While Bedard is currently under contract with the Chicago Blackhawks, he will become a restricted free agent in 2026. Restricted free agency provides the team with certain rights to match any offer made to the player by another team, ensuring that the Blackhawks have the opportunity to retain Bedard's services.

Performance-Based Incentives

In addition to his base salary and signing bonus, Bedard has the potential to earn up to $3.5 million in incentives or performance-based payments throughout his contract. These incentives are divided into two categories: Type "A" and Type "B" performance. Type "A" performance incentives are based on Bedard's goals, assists, points, ice time, and All-Star selections. Type "B" performance incentives depend on top rankings in statistical categories and awards.

Career Earnings Projection

If Bedard achieves all of his performance-based bonuses, his career NHL earnings could reach $13,500,000 by the 2025-26 season. These incentives provide Bedard with the opportunity to further increase his earnings, reflecting his exceptional skills and potential as a player.
