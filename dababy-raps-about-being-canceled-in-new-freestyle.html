DaBaby Raps About Being 'Canceled' In New Freestyle

Publish date: 2024-03-16

In a new freestyle for LA Leakers DaBaby rapped about recently being canceled.

“I slap a grown-ass man in front of all of his n*ggas, let’s see who the bitch now, all out the window/ I know we supposed to be mad at DaBaby, and that n*gga canceled, but that n*gga lit, and these n*gga hoes. I came from the bottom, I didn’t have sh*t wasn’t even supposed to see this.”

He later raps, “N*ggas talking about DaBaby on the blogs, n*gga so what? Do something, I’m pleading/ I ain’t have to show me a n*gga what’s happening in a while, trigger-finger motherf*cka squeezing.”

DaBaby was almost canceled after making homophobic statements at the Rolling Loud Miami Festival last year.

“[If] you didn’t show up today with HIV/AIDS, or any of them deadly sexually transmitted diseases that’ll make you die in two to three weeks, then put a cellphone light in the air,” he said. “Ladies, if your p*ssy smell like water, put a cellphone light in the air. Fellas, if you ain’t sucking d*ck in the parking lot, put a cellphone light in the air.”
