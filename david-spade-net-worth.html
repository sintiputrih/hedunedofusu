David Spade is a well-known comedian and actor who has appeared in numerous television shows and films throughout his career. Spade has owned several impressive homes in Los Angeles, including a property in Beverly Hills that he sold for $19.5 million in 2022. He currently has residences in West Hollywood and the Hollywood Hills, the latter of which he purchased for $13.85 million in 2021.
