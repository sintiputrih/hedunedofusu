Meet Diane T. Williams, Porsha Williams Mom

Ms. Diane or Miss Diane or Diane Thompson Williams whatever you call is the mother of the RHOA star Porsha Williams.

Diane has played a significant role in the life of all of her children, most importantly Porsha.

Porsha’s fans have commended Diane for her young appearance, her entrepreneurial path, and being a loving grandmother.

Diane T. Williams Age

Diane T. Williams is 63 years old as of this writing in November 2021. She recently celebrated her birthday on 24 January 2021.

Diane T. Williams Husband

Diane T. Williams was previously married to Hosea William II. Her husband, Hosea was the son of Hosea Williams Sr., a civil rights leader in Martin Luther King Jr.’s inner circle, while his mother, Juanita, was a member of the Georgia House of Representatives.

According to the memorial page, Hosea Jr. died in 1998 of complications from a bone marrow transplant, after being diagnosed with a rare form of leukemia the year prior. He passed away at the age of 43. He attended Morehouse College and the University of Georgia Law School.

Hosea Jr. had four children, Porsha Williams, Lauren Williams, and two sons Hosea Williams III, and Brenton Williams. While Hosea Jr.’s famous daughters have spoken and paid tribute to him on their social media, Diane speaks and shared relatively less about her late husband.

Diane is currently in a relationship with a man named Larry. He was first introduced to the world by Porsha Williams on her Instagram in December 2020, on his birthday. Porsha wrote, “🎉👴🏿Happy Birthday Larry !! We love you so much !! Pjs Pop pop! #DianesMan.”

Not only that but Porsha also penned a special message to Larry from her daughter Pilar Jhena. She captioned, “Happy Birthday Pop Pop!! I love you !! I wish you many many more!!!” The post was a clip of the duo lovingly sharing a piece of cake.

On the other hand, Diane also gushed about her love for Larry. She posted a short clip where they danced. She captioned the post, “Happy Birthday baby I love you forever and a day! 🥳 #Larry #EnjoyLife #Love”

On Christmas day 2020, the in-love couple again appeared in a short clip that Diane posted on her IG. She wrote, “I’m not internationally known but I’m known to rock a microphone 🎶 #MerryChristmas”

Larry appeared on Diane’s post for the first time in May 2020 on the mother’s day video clip.

Is Diane T. Williams On IG?

Yes, Diane is on Instagram. She gathered 144k followers on her Instagram @msdianeofficial. She is also on Facebook.

Diane T. Williams Job: What Does She Do For A Living?

Diane T. Williams’s IG bio revealed that she is an entrepreneur and public speaker. She and her late husband’s entrepreneurial nature was what inspired Porsha to follow the same path.

In an interview with Jet in 2014, Porsha revealed, “My mother was an entrepreneur and so was my father, so it just was in my blood to want to own something for myself. Before I became a wife [to ex-husband Kordell Stewart] I had my own business, a childcare center, that I started when I was 24 years old. My mother was in the field, so I saved up money from previous jobs and got my location. You can be spoiled and still know how to handle business.”

Diane runs a daycare business. She was 30 years old when she owned her first 24-hour childcare center in Atlanta. Now, she is taking her talent to look after her grandchildren.

Furthermore, Diane is also a TV personality appearing on “The Real Housewives of Atlanta.” On the regular show, she appeared on 27 episodes and reunion episodes part 1 and part 2 in 2020 and 2021 respectively.
