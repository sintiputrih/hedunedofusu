Does Al Michael have a glass eye?

Publish date: 2024-03-04

Alan Richard Michaels was born on November 12, 1944, in Brooklyn, New York. Michael is an American television sportscaster and works for NBC Sports after he spent nearly three decades from 1976 to 2006) with ABC Sports. Michaels is known for his many years of calling play-by-play of National Football League games.

Michael has received several awards and honors including

  • Five-time Sports Emmy Award winner – Outstanding Sports Personality, Play-by-Play
  • Three-time NSMA National Sportscaster of the Year
  • Sportscaster of the Year – American Sportscasters Association (ASA)
  • Sportscaster of the Year – Washington Journalism Review
  • ASA Top 50 Sportscasters of All Time.
  • Walter Cronkite Award for Excellence in Journalism (2002).
  • Pacific Pioneer Broadcasters “Art Gilmore Career Achievement Award” June 16, 2017.

Does Al Michael have a glass eye?

Michael has an eye condition that causes his left eye to drift. But his disabilities haven’t impacted his career arc as a broadcaster. However, we can’t fully pledge that Michael has a glass eye.
