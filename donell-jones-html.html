Donell Jones - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-17

R&B singer who released the Platinum-selling album Where I Wanna Be in 1999. He recorded the single music for Love and Basketball called "I'll Go." 

Before Fame

He signed his first record contract with LaFace Records in 1996. 

Trivia

He earned his first #1 R&B single with "U Know What's Up" in 1999. The video for that song has earned over 42 million views on YouTube. As a songwriter, he has worked with stars like Usher. Aside from a few songs, every album he has released has been written and produced by him. 

Family Life

He began a relationship with girlfriend Jasmine in 2007 and the two wed in 2014. He's fathered five daughters and one son.

Associated With

He covered Stevie Wonder's hit song, "Knocks Me Off My Feet," in 1996. He has also toured with artists like Alicia Keys.
