Dont touch my stomach with your stomach, cuz thats gonna be a problem: Teenager challeng

Publish date: 2024-01-25

A prank and skit TikToker is going viral for challenging a police officer to a brawl. The video depicts the creator testing the officer’s limits and uttering unhinged taunts with a straight face.

Recommended Videos

The TikToker, who goes by Trey Da Menace on TikTok, tells the officer “You won’t step right here,” and “How are you my height? I’m 19,” which provoke profanity-laden reactions out of the police officer. Which was likely exactly what Trey was hoping for.

It seems as though the point of the video is that Trey is “using old man quotes,” like “you’re on thin ice,” to get under the officer’s skin. He captioned the video, “Bro was ready to brawl.”

His audience seemed to find the video hilarious as well, since it earned over 7 million views and 9 thousand comments.

Commenters seem to be goading Trey on, saying “Bro has mastered escalating and de escalating at the exact same time” and “Bro was using his own dialogue against him.” Followers seemed to enjoy Trey pulling out police language and tactics to try and get a rise out of the police officer.

Trey Da Menace is known for getting into this kind of trouble. He frequently posts videos of arguments with fast food workers, security guards, and law enforcement officers. His content revolves around catching professionals being angry, aggressive, or unprofessional after he tries to aggravate them.

Trey has earned more than 150 thousand followers this way as well as 25.9 thousand YouTube subscribers. Fans waiting to see who Trey gets riled up next can follow along on his TikTok and Youtube Channel.
