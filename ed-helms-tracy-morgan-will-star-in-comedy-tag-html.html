Ed Helms and Tracy Morgan Will Star in Comedy TAG

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Ed Helms and Tracy Morgan will both be starring in the film TAG, reports Variety. Broad City’s Jeff Tomsic will direct the film, which is based on the true story of a group of friends who play cross-country tag for 30 years. It is based on a feature that ran in The Wall Street Journal in 2013 that followed ten classmates from Gonzaga Preparatory School in Spokane, Washington. After attending school together, the friends moved across the country, but still managed to play their own version of tag, and played it at family vacations. Mark Steilen and Rob McKittrick are writing the script, and Todd Garner and Sean Robins will produce with Steilen.
