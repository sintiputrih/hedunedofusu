Ernest Keith Smith - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-14

American country singer who initially became known for country rap singles like "Dopeman" and "Bad Boy." He released his debut album Locals Only in 2019 and followed up in 2022 with Flower Shops (The Album). He has written songs for several well-known country artists like Chris Lane and Jake Owen.

Before Fame

He got into music through an interest in hip-hop, with Eminem being one of his early influences.

Trivia

His 2022 single "Flower Shops" has racked up more than a hundred million streams on Spotify. He shares moment's in his day-to-day life with his family on his ernest Instagram account, where he has gained upwards of 450,000 followers.

Family Life

He has a son named Ryman.

Associated With

He collaborated with Morgan Wallen on "Flower Shops."
