Biography

Femi Adebayo (born 31 December 1972) is an energetic and highly influential Nigerian actor, director, producer, businessman, and politician.

He has achieved what most other actors in Nigeria haven’t.

Little wonder he was appointed as the special adviser to the governor of Kwara on arts, culture, and Tourism.

He burst into the celebrity caucus when he acted in the Yoruba comedy film, Jelili and Ladies Gang.

Early Life

Femi Adebayo is the son of legendary and renowned Nigerian actor, Adebayo Salami also known as Oga Bello.

He was born on 31 December 1972 in Western Nigeria, Lagos, to be precise.

He grew up in a polygamous home with 12 siblings.

Notable of his siblings are Tope Adebayo, Lai Adebayo, Kemi Adebayo, Sadiq Adebayo, and Omolara Adebayo.

Femi is Yoruba by ethnicity.

As a young lad, his father put him through acting and performance.

He was keen and passionate about becoming a successful actor just like Father or even better.

Femi Adebayo‘s passion and keen interest paid off as a director contacted him to act his first film at thirteen.

Education

The respected actor, Femi Adebayo studied at the C&S college located in Lagos before gaining admission into the prestigious University of Ilorin situated in Kwara state Nigeria.

At the University of Ilorin, Femi Adebayo pursued and graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Law.

The entrepreneur furthers his education by attaining a master’s degree in Law at the University of Ibadan, Oyo state.

Career

Femi Adebayo began his journey in the movie industry in 1985 when he was just 13 years old. He acted in a movie produced by his father titled, Ogun Ajaye. The super actor has gone on to star in over 200 Nollywood movies.

Femi Adebayo deemed it necessary to improve the movie industry in Nigeria, thus creating a film arts school. He went on to established the J-15 Media in Ibadan. J-15 Media is a school of performing arts.

His good works and contributions to film and arts development in Nigeria were significant grounds for his appointment by the Governor of Kwara state as the special adviser on arts, culture, and Tourism.

Personal Life
