The 22-year-old daughter of renowned boxer, Floyd Mayweather, has been given a sentence following a violent altercation in 2020.

Court documents indicated that Iyanna Mayweather pleaded guilty to stabbing Jazlyn Mychelle. The incident occurred at the residence of her child’s father, NBA YoungBoy, on April 4, 2020.

At 19 years old, Mayweather admitted to an aggravated assault with a deadly weapon charge. Subsequently, she was granted Deferred Adjudication of Guilt and received a six-year probation sentence.

A representative for the Harris County Precinct 4 Constable’s Office revealed that Iyanna, also known as “Yaya,” was arrested for felony aggravated assault with a deadly weapon at a Houston residence around 1 a.m. local time on a Saturday morning and was taken to Houston’s Harris County jail. She was later released on bond.

The victim, Jazlyn Mychelle, a 35-year-old black female, sustained multiple lacerations to her arm and was transported to a hospital via ambulance for non-life-threatening injuries. After undergoing surgery, she is currently in stable condition.

TMZ reported that Mayweather confronted YoungBoy and the victim at his Houston home, claiming to be YoungBoy’s fiancee. In the ensuing confrontation in the kitchen, Mayweather reportedly assaulted the victim with two knives.

In 2021, Yaya welcomed a child with Youngboy. However, their relationship ended around the same time. Youngboy has since had multiple children with Jazlyn Mychelle. Presently, the 23-year-old Louisiana rapper has a total of 11 children.
