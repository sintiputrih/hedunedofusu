Galina Mikhaylovna Loginova Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Galina Jovovich née Loginova (born October 28, 1950 in Tuapse, Soviet Russia) is a Russian actress. She was famous in the Soviet Union for her roles in movies, and then gained additional popularity for being the agent for her daughter, actress and model Milla Jovovich. She currently acts in American and Russian movies.Most recently she appeared in the American silent movie Silent Life, alongside Vlad Kozlov and Isabella Rossellini, based on the life of Hollywood icon Rudolph Valentino. She is portraying legendary Hollywood silent movie star Alla Nazimova in a film set for release in 2014. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
