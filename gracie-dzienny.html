Gracie Dzienny is an American actress and model who is best known for having been cast as Amanda McKay in the Nickelodeon action-comedy superhero series, Supah Ninjas, alongside the likes of Ryan Potter, Carlos Knight, and George Takei. The show won Writers Guild of America Award in 2012 in the “Children’s – Episodic & Specials” category as well as the 65th Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Award in the “Outstanding Stunt Coordination for a Comedy Series or a Variety Program” category in 2013. She also appeared as Greer Danville in the ABC Family’s romantic drama series, Chasing life. Apart from these, Gracie Dzienny has appeared in a number of shows which include Home & Family, Collab Lab, Side Effects, State of Affairs, See Dad Run, Fred: The Show, Up All Night, Zoo, Ramy, ATVN Collabs, and AwesomenessTV. Gracie was also cast as Tina Lark, Charlie’s mean-spirited classmate, in the science fiction action adventure film, Bumblebee, in 2018. She started modeling at the age of 5 after she won a competition sponsored by the popular brand, L’Oréal, and later, she also moved to New York for some modeling assignments and was also a part of a couple of commercials. Moreover, her love for baking is also no hidden treasure as the star used to frequently prepare cupcakes for her Supah Ninjas cast and crew while working on the show. Gracie Dzienny has also garnered a huge social media fan base with her acting finesse and enjoys more than 80k followers on Twitter, more than 80k followers on Instagram, and more than 20k followers on Facebook.
