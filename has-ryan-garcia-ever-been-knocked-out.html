Has Ryan Garcia ever been knocked out?

Publish date: 2024-10-02

Ryan Garcia, a prominent figure in the world of boxing, has had a remarkable career journey that has seen victories, setbacks, and a continuous quest for greatness. The question of whether Ryan Garcia has ever been knocked out is a relevant one, considering his standing as a talented boxer. To provide a thorough analysis, let's delve into Garcia's career, notable fights, and the instances where he has faced defeat.

Early Career and Rise Up the Ranks

Ryan Garcia began his professional boxing career at the age of 17 in 2016, quickly gaining attention for his talent and skills in the ring. He signed with Golden Boy Promotions and embarked on a journey that would see him rise up the ranks as a promising young boxer.

Garcia vs. Duno

In September 2019, Garcia faced Romero Duno and secured a first-round knockout victory, capturing the WBC Silver lightweight title.

Garcia vs. Fonseca

Following his win against Duno, Garcia went on to face Francisco Fonseca in February 2020 and delivered a stunning first-round knockout, further solidifying his position as a rising star in the boxing world.

WBC Interim Lightweight Champion

One of the most significant moments in Garcia's career came when he faced Olympic gold medalist Luke Campbell for the interim WBC lightweight title in January 2021. Despite being knocked down for the first time in his professional career during the fight, Garcia displayed resilience and eventually secured a technical knockout victory over Campbell, earning him the interim WBC champion title.

Notable Fights and Defeats

Defeat against Gervonta Davis

In April 2022, Garcia faced Gervonta Davis in a highly anticipated bout. However, this match resulted in a seventh-round knockout loss for Garcia, marking the first defeat of his professional career.

Return to the Ring and Victories

Following his loss to Davis, Garcia bounced back with victories over notable opponents such as Javier Fortuna and Oscar Duarte, showcasing his determination and skill in the ring.

Injury and Fight Postponement

Garcia's journey also faced challenges when he suffered a hand injury, leading to the postponement of a scheduled fight with Joseph Diaz Jr.

Has Ryan Garcia Ever Been Knocked Out?

While Ryan Garcia has not been knocked out in the majority of his professional career, the bout against Gervonta Davis in April 2022 resulted in a seventh-round knockout loss for Garcia. This defeat stands as a significant moment in Garcia's career, highlighting the unpredictable nature of boxing and the resilience required to navigate the highs and lows of the sport. Despite this setback, Garcia remains a formidable talent in the boxing world, with a record that reflects both victories and the determination to continue pursuing excellence in the ring.

With a career marked by notable victories, setbacks, and a relentless pursuit of success, Ryan Garcia's journey in boxing continues to capture the attention of fans and enthusiasts alike. His ability to overcome challenges and display exceptional skill in the ring reinforces his status as a prominent figure in the sport. As he continues to navigate his career, it is evident that Garcia's story in boxing is far from over, and his resilience in the face of defeat only adds to the compelling narrative of his professional journey.

Ryan Garcia has experienced a knockout defeat in his professional career, but his overall prowess and determination position him as a boxer with the potential for future triumphs and continued success in the sport.
