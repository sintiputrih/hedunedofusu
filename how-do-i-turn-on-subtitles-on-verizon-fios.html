Where is the closed caption button on FIOS remote?

Most FiberOptic TV remote control models let you turn Closed Captioning on or off by pressing the (asterisk) button. Otherwise, you can always use the Media Guide to turn Closed Captioning on or off. On your remote control: Select Menu > Settings > System > Accessibility > Close Captions.
