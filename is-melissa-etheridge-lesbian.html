Is Melissa Etheridge lesbian?

Publish date: 2024-06-12

Melissa Lou Etheridge is a singer, songwriter, musician, and guitarist from the United States. Etheridge’s debut album, eponymous, was released in 1988 and became an underground success.

It reached No. 22 on the Billboard 200, and its lead single, “Bring Me Some Water,” earned Etheridge her first Grammy Award nomination in 1989 for Best Rock Vocal Performance, Female.

How did Melissa Etheridge come out?

Etheridge came out as a lesbian at the Triangle Ball, a gay celebration of President Bill Clinton’s first inauguration, in January 1993. Etheridge supported Clinton’s presidential campaign in 1992 and has been a gay rights activist since coming out.
