Jordin Sparks Stuns in Bikini at Las Vegas Event With Jason Derulo

Publish date: 2024-02-27

A long way from the American Idol stage! Jordin Sparks sizzled on the red carpet at the grand opening of the Tao Beach season at The Venetian in Las Vegas on Saturday, April 19. 

The recording artist, 24, looked fit and trim in a neon two-piece Pan Dulce suit with sky-high Louboutin wedges. Boyfriend Jason Derulo was by her side in a black tank top with red shorts and sneakers.

Though she looked like a pro on the carpet, Sparks admitted that getting there was a challenging road.
