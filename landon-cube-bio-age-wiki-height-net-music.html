Biography and age of Landon Cube

Musical career

His passion for music resurfaced in 4th grade, and eventually he picked up the guitar and started learning the basics under the watchful eye of his father – a professional musician who he says is his inspiration. In 2013, he shared his music videos on YouTube with his songs including American Dream, Euphoria and Victim. He hit SoundCloud three years later with several of his songs uploaded to the channel in 2016. Landon Cube then took off with his May 2017 single Beachtown. He wasted no time in releasing other singles like 19, Legend and Mad of the New World which followed the same year. Landon’s single Driving My Car appeared in January 2018 and the same year he contributed to Louis Luv’s song Quel est le Luv. Driving My Car and Beachtown leading in the most watched video stakes on its Youtube channel. Along with Lil Skies, he was featured in No Jumper – a September 2016 podcast by Adam22.
