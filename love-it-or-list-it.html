'Love It or List It' News and Updates

Publish date: 2024-05-13

America is definitely obsessed with the Canadian home design series Love It or List It. The premise is simple: a couple must decide whether they want to stay in their existing home — that gets a complete renovation by interior designer Hilary Farr — or put it up for sale and get a new home with the help of real estate agent David Visentin. And in case you are wondering, Hilary usually wins.
