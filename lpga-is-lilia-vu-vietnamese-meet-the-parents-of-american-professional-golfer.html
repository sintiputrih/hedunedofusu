LPGA: Is Lilia Vu Vietnamese?

Lilla Vu is an American professional golfer with Vietnamese roots. Both of the LPGA star’s parents have Vietnamese origin.

The California-born golfer’s maternal grandfather immigrated with his family to the United States in 1982 from Vietnam during the Vietnamese war.

Her grandpa had a huge contribution and sacrifice to move to the US during the raging war. He built the boat himself and took as many people as he could in the sinking ship before an American ship picked them up.
