If you are a fan of sports anchoring, you probably know about the sizzling and talented Molly Qerim. The 35-year-old is a sports anchor and moderator for ESPN’s First Take, which fruits her with a commanding salary and net worth.

Behind the camera lenses, Molly Qerim leads a wonderful married life. She is proud to be a better half of the former professional basketball player and ESPN’s sports analyst husband.

Molly Qerim’s Bio: Age, Height, Education

The 36-years-of-age media personality was born as Molly Qerim Rose on 31 March 1984 in New Haven, Connecticut. She shares half Italian and half Albanian ancestry.

As of her educational background, Molly attended the University of Connecticut where she graduated with a degree in Communication and a minor in business administration. Subsequently, she received a master’s degree in broadcast journalism from Quinnipiac University.

Molly stands at a tall height of 5 feet and 7 inches (1.68 m) and weighs around 63 kg.

Her Career As Sports Reporter

Molly Qerim took center stage as a sports analyst reporting for CBS Sports Network. There, she covered college football, National Signing Day, the NCCA Tournament, and the U.S. Open. Besides, she also presented SEC Tailgate Show, SEC Tonight, and Bracket Breakdown as a studio anchor.

Additionally, Molly also covered the UFC in her time with the NBCSN and FS1. She also served as the host of NFL Networks’ weekday morning show, NFL AM, and NFL Fantasy Live. Meanwhile, she was the interactive host for College Football Live on ESPN and ESPN2.

Molly Qerim anchoring ESPN’s First Take (Picture: ESPN Front Row)

The sports moderator took center stage as the interim host of ESPN’s sports talk show, First Take in mid-July 2015. She later got promotion as the permanent host of the show on 15 September 2015. Prior to that, Molly covered Super Bowls, NBA Draft, NBA All-Star Game and the MLB All-Star Game.

More on Molly’s Sporting Career and Accolades

Molly was also the breaking news reporter for Fantasy Football Now on ESPN2 and was honored with an Emmy for her contribution to the show. Additionally, she co-hosted Campus Connection on ESPNU.

Moreover, she has been a part of other digital media content, conducting interviews of various athletes and celebrities for ESPN.com and ESPN Mobile. She has covered multiple Super Bowls (hosting, reporting, and red carpet events), and the Heisman Trophy presentation.

Her Salary & Net Worth

For her astounding sports reporting, Molly Qerim earns quite a remarkable paycheck. As per some reports, her average annual salary at ESPN swirls around $52K. Apart from that, her net worth figure dwells above $4 Million. 

Molly Qerim’s Married Life With Jalen Rose; Co-Parent Three Kids

The talented and hot sports moderator is married to a fellow sports analyst for ESPN, Jalen Rose. Jalen is also a former professional basketball player, who retired in 2007 after a 16-season-long NBA stint. He is also a co-founder of the Jalen Rose Leadership Academy.

Molly Qerim with her husband Jalen Rose on 9 May 2019 (Picture: Molly’s Instagram)

Molly and Jalen walked down the aisle together in July 2018. The duo confirmed their relationship back in 2016, sometime after Molly began posting photos of her and Jalen on her social sites. Jalen has three kids from his previous relationship which includes two daughters, Mariah and Gracie and a son named LaDarius.
