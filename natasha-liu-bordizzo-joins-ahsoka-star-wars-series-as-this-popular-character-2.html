Natasha Liu Bordizzo Joins Ahsoka Star Wars Series As This Popular Character!

Publish date: 2024-02-06

Natasha Liu Bordizzo is part of the Star Wars world!

The 27-year-old actress has joined the upcoming Ahsoka series, opposite Rosario Dawson, as fan favorite Sabine, Deadline reports.

Sabine Wren, who many fans know from the animated Star Wars Rebels, is a young Mandalorian warrior and graffiti artist, Imperial Academy dropout and a former bounty hunter with expert knowledge of weapons and explosives.

The character was voiced by Tiya Sircar in the animated show.

Ahsoka will center on the character, which also made their debut in the Rebels series, following the events of her appearance on The Mandalorian.

It’s rumored that Hayden Christensen, as Anakin Skywalker, will also appear in the show.
