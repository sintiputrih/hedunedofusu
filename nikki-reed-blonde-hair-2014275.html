Nikki Reed Debuts Blonde Hair: See the Makeover Pictures

Publish date: 2024-04-07

In the long tradition of changing one's hairstyle after a breakup, Nikki Reed debuted a lighter look on May 23, just days after filing for divorce from Paul McDonald. 

Keeping the length of her formerly brown hair, the Twilight actress, 26, lightened the shade to a honey-toned blonde.

Reed teased her new color via Instagram on Friday, May 23—hinting that the change is actually for a role. "Finishing touches," she wrote. "Less than a week away from filming #TheSundayHorse."

In The Sunday Horse, based on a true story, Reed plays Debi Walden, an equestrian rider who overcomes a near-fatal accident to win the U.S. championship.
