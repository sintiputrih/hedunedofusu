See Photos Of Matt Damon &amp; More Stars Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-01-16

Matt Damon, Robert Downey Jr, Cillian Murphy, Emily Blunt and Christopher Nolan are a lineup of superstars at the highly anticipated premiere of ‘Oppenheimer’ at Grand Rex on July 11, 2023, in Paris, France. The epic biopic, which chronicles the life and achievements of theoretical physicist J. Robert Oppenheimer, stars Cillian Murphy in the title role, with A-listers Emily Blunt and Matt Damon starring opposite. 

Emily, who previously starred in Disney’s ‘Jungle Book,’ looked stunning in a silk mint green gown with a high neckline and distinctive collar. The skirt, gathered at the side, gave the dress a retro flair, and she channeled old Hollywood glamour in deep, shiny curls. 

Matt opted for a simple blue suit paired with a clean white tee shirt and brown shoes, while Cillian rocked a black suit with a chic beige button down shirt and monochromatic beige tie. Robert Downey Jr, ever the fashion-conscious actor, rocked head to toe black and a pair of sunglasses for the big event. The film’s director, Nolan, wore a gray suit paired with a black tie.
