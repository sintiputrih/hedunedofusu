Park Jung-ho On Netflix’s Physical 100

Park Jung-ho joined a whopping 100 cast members of Netflix Korea’s Physical 100.

The show is Netflix’s first Korean survival show. On January 24, 2023, the first two episodes on the streaming service were released, and a lot of viewers tuned in to see what it was all about. If the top 10 TV shows on Netflix are any indication, Physical: 100 is doing very well. It is currently at No. 7 and doesn’t appear to be dropping off any time soon as of Jan. 26.

Jang Ho-gi is the creator of the reality competition program Physical: 100. There are nine episodes altogether, and each Tuesday, two new episodes are released on Netflix. (Valium) The program follows 100 competitors with varying ages, genders, and physical characteristics. They engage in intense competition with one another to demonstrate their physical prowess and for the chance to claim the title of “most perfect physique.”

Of course, the winner will also receive a 300,000,000 (WON) cash prize. The cast includes members of the national team, weight lifters, actresses, bodybuilders, ice climbers, influencers, TV personalities, MMA fighters, and others from a variety of professions. In fact, you might be able to identify a few of the competitors.

Nam Koung-jin, an unlimited heavyweight wrestler, chooses the jail guard to compete against him in the one-on-one death battle. One of the other competitors wondered aloud if there would be a fatality in the battle between the two heavyweights. In order to achieve a victory, Jung-ho had to think tactically. To ensure that the wrestler couldn’t get to the ball, he made the astute decision to toss it to the top of the blocks.

Unfortunately for him, the maneuver was prohibited since it took the action outside of the arena. The prison guard was then easily lifted off the ground by the wrestler, who had no mercy as he did so.

The guard was then further beaten and “fold him over like a piece of paper” Park Jung-ho finally had enough of everything. After being defeated, the jail guard was seen crying as he had to demolish his sculptured torso to leave the game.

After his journey ended in the show, he posted on IG and explained that he did his best but understood he lacked a lot. But he attested that “Korean prison guards aren’t weak.” He had previously announced his appearance by writing, “Netflix Physical 100 coming soon~ A dream-like experience with amazing people~ I was happy and honored to be able to be with them~ Please watch a lot ♡.”

Park Jung-ho Job

Park Jung-ho is a prison guard or correction officer by trade. He has been working that job for more than 17 years. He initially guarded dangerous criminals including Sin Chang-won, Yoo Yung-chul, and Kang Ho-son in addition to well-known hazardous offenders.

According to him, the majority of prisoners have bodies that go beyond those of regular people. He needs to be physically capable enough to defend himself and keep other prisoners at bay there. the capacity to force prisoners to submit.

He has also posted a pic of his first term with an old uniform that new officers are unfamiliar with. Based on a post from August 2022, he is working at Suwon Detention Center.

In July 2022, Jung-ho updated his IG about a new start. He shared that he was returning to the frontlines after finishing his life at headquarters in the Ministry of Justice. Furthermore, then he has also posted about fire training and anti-terrorism training that he and his team took in August 2022.

In addition to his regular jobs, Jung-ho appeared on several TV shows. He participated in the tvN You Quiz on Double Block Episode 68. He also appeared on Ktv Latte Classic Season 3. One of his life goals is to be a good influencer.

Fun Fact: Back in 2016, Jung-ho also participated in a judo competition of Korea-Japan Prison Officer Martial Arts.
