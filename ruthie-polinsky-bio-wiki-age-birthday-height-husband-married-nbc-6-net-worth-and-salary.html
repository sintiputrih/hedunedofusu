Ruthie Polinsky NBC 6

Polinsky is currently a sports anchor and reporter for NBC 6 in Miami, Florida covering the top stories and breaking news from across the area and hosting Sunday Sports Final. She joined the NBC 6 sports team after serving three years as a sports anchor and reporter at WPRI in Providence, Rhode Island.

“I am ecstatic to join the talented team at NBC6 – a station rich in history, and one that centers around the philosophy of connecting on a deeply personal level with the South Florida community,” Polinsky said.

During her period in New England, Polinsky covered two Super Bowls with the New England Patriots, a World Series championship with the Boston Red Sox, the Boston Celtics Eastern Conference Finals, and a Stanley Cup Final run with the Boston Bruins (in her hometown of St. Louis). She additionally covered an NCAA Tournament trip with the Providence Friars and URI Rams.

Ruthie Polinsky KFDX

Polinsky was the weekend sports anchor and reporter in Wichita Falls, Texas at KFDX prior to her life in the Ocean State, where she covered a number of high school sports, but mostly high school football.

Ruthie also covered the sports teams at Midwestern State, an NCAA Division II university, in Wichita Falls, where she saw both football and men’s basketball win conference championships.
