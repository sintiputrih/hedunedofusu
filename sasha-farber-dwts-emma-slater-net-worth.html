'DWTS' Suggest Sasha Farber And His Wife Emma Slater To Live Separately Due To COVID-19 Pandemic

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Everyone in the world needs to be safe. Concerning the ongoing pandemic Coronavirus (COVID-19), health safety measures and guidelines have come forth in every field, including entertainment, business, politics, adventures, games, and others. And Dancing With The Stars is just a part of that.

With that, the contestants of the season 29 of the DWTS will be separated, which has shattered the excitement of the Russian professional dancer and his fiancee, Emma Slater. The lovebirds, who got engaged in 2016 and tied their knot in 2018, are suggested to live separately during their competition on Dancing With The Stars, as one of the safety measures to be safe from COVID-19.

Sasha Farber in Dancing With The Stars (©: HeadlinePlanet)

Speaking about the show's guidelines regarding the pandemic, Emma Slater talked to Entertainment Tonight and mentioned, 'Everybody needs this right now. It's such an uplifting show, and we're so incredibly proud to be part of it.' She added:

"But it's so funny. When our amazing producers told us, 'OK, so we have some news for you, you're going to have to be quarantined separately,' I laughed. I was nervous, so I just started laughing. But we came to the executive decision that I would be the one moving out because..."

"She would burn the house down. All my flowers would die, the dog would die, the house would be a mess," Sasha joked. "But it's really important to do these things and it's great that producers thought of this. There's lots of things happening -- masks, social distancing -- and this is one way of making sure that everyone's safe, strong and the show goes on. Because we need this show!"

Nevertheless, the lovebirds are staying apart throughout their time on the dancing franchise show. Also, she said that the temporary move is happening soon.

Likewise, Sasha Farber claimed that producers' changes might stick around after the pandemic is over. All the contestants will be under observation under the camera are not allowed to go into another's room, the new rule says.

Married Life Of Sasha Farber and Emma Slater

Sasha Farber and Emma Slater are married for a couple of years. After dating for a few years, they had a wedding on 25 March 2018 in Los Angeles in a star-studded ceremony attended by their fellow dancers. Witney Carson and Lindsay Arnold served as a bridesmaid, and Derek Hough served as one of the groomsmen.

Emma Slater donned in a mermaid gown with a smooth skirt of crepe silk and long-sleeve lace bodice while Farber accompanied her in a cream tuxedo jacket. Slater shared the insight of her wedding on Instagram, where she wrote:

"We are beyond excited to wake up this morning as Mr & Mrs Farber! I’m so overwhelmed right now, I know I’ll share more photos but for now I just wanted to thank everyone from the bottom of our hearts for all your kind and beautiful messages of love."

She added, 'Yesterday was hands down the best day of my life, I’m married to the man of my dreams ❤️'

Their wedding came after a couple of years after they got engaged on the stage of Dancing With The Stars in 2016. Farber went down on his knees and proposed the love of his life flaunting an engagement ring.'

It all happened after they performed a contemporary dance in Bruno Mars' Just the Way You Are, and Slater said 'Yes.' The couple then shared an intimate kiss, after which all the set congratulated the newly engaged pair.

How Much Is Sasha Farber's Net Worth?

According to the source, Sasha Farber has an estimated net worth of around $300 thousand. He is speculated to earn his fortune from his professional career as a dancer. The 5 foot 7 inches dancer is a two-times winner of the Australian Latin Championships at the World Latin Championship.

Similarly, he appeared in the fourteenth season of the Dancing With The Stars. And he is again competing in the 29th season of DWTS with his lovely wife, Emma Slater, this year.
