Who is Steve Belichick? Bill Belichick Son

Steve Belichick is the son of a renowned American NFL coach and athletic personality, William Stephen Belichick, widely recognized as Bill Belichick.

Interestingly, the father-son duo work for the same team in the NFL. Steve is the linebacker coach and assistant, whereas his father is the head coach of the New England Patriots.

Steve is one of the 3 children of his father Bill Belichick and his mother Debby Clarke Belichick. Meaning, he has 2 siblings, sister Amanda Belichick and brother Brian Belichick.

Currently, his parents are not together since they got divorced about 15 years ago, after nearly 30 years of marriage.

Meet Steve Belichick Wife

Steve Belichick is married to his wife Jennifer Schmitt since June of 2017.

They dated for 10 years and then got married, and in these years of togetherness, they gave birth to 2 children, Blakely Rose Belichick, and Hayes William Belichick.
