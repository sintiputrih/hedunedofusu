Taylor Swift Allegedly Rejected Meghan Markle As Experts Claim She Chose William And Kate's Side

Publish date: 2024-01-12

Highlights

  • Royal commentator claims Meghan Markle sent a handwritten letter to Taylor Swift but was rejected.
  • Taylor Swift thought to be siding with Prince William and Kate Middleton in their feud with Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.
  • Travis Kelce praised Prince William after meeting him, further fueling rumors of Swift's allegiance.

Rumors are going around that Taylor Swift is siding with Prince William and Kate Middleton in their feud with Prince Harry and Meghan Markle. The heir to the throne attended one of Taylor Swift’s Wembley Stadium Eras tour concerts and the pop star posted a picture of herself and Travis Kelce with the family, wishing the prince a happy birthday.

Prince William and his children looked beyond excited to be there, and she was seemingly honored to have them attend her show. Does that mean she is turning her back on Harry and Meghan? This royal commentator certainly seems to think so.

Taylor Swift Reportedly Turned Down Meghan Markle

After Taylor Swift's post for Prince William's birthday, royal experts became convinced that she is choosing sides in one of the biggest divides in the royal family feud. This rumor was fueled when royal commentator Kinsey Schofield revealed that Meghan Markle had sent a note to Taylor, attempting to establish a relationship, but was politely turned down.

"She wrote her a handwritten letter and Taylor's team rejected it," the expert explained. "Megan also later released through People magazine that she had spent some time at a Taylor Swift concert. So I think we are seeing our Taylor side with Team Wales."

The royal commentator also expanded on Taylor Swift and Prince William’s great rapport.

"I think that he's [Prince William] probably spent a few minutes with her [Taylor]. We know that they took a selfie. She has spent time with him previously, there is a great video of them singing with Jon Bon Jovi at a charity event."

Kinsey Schofield commented on the comparison between Prince William and Prince Harry’s attitudes at concerts as well.

“A lot of people pointing out the pictures of Meghan and Harry at the Beyoncé concert, where Harry looks pretty miserable versus Prince William shaking his tail feather at the Taylor Swift concert. You're just seeing that Prince William is pursuing joy. And I think that is important," the expert concluded.

Travis Kelce Praised Prince William After Meeting Him

Travis and Jason Kelce, who of course were at Taylor's show, also got to meet Prince William and were admittedly delighted with him. Travis did not hold back on complimenting the heir to the throne, and Jason completely agreed with him.

"[Prince William] was the coolest motherf***er. They were an absolute delight to me. I wasn’t sure if I was supposed to bow to them or curtsy, or just be an American idiot and shake their hand," he laughed during a New Heights episode.

Jason joined in, saying that his favorite part was meeting Prince William and Kate Middleton's children, especially Princess Charlotte.

"Prince George was great too, but she was so f***ing adorable,” he gushed. “I cannot express how much of a superstar she was. Maybe it’s because I have three girls now, but she had fire to her, she was asking questions. That was the most electric part of it."

It might be an exaggeration to say they are choosing sides, but it's clear that Taylor and Travis have a great relationship with Prince William, regardless of what that means to Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.
