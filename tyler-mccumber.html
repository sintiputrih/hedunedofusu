Tyler McCumber is an up-and-coming professional golfer from the United States. Tyler McCumber has been playing professionally for about a decade. Likewise, he is a member of the PGA Tour.

Early Lifestyle and Childhood

Tyler McCumber was born Mark Tyler McCumber in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida, on April 4, 1991. Mark McCumber and his wife Paddy McCumber are his parents. His father is a professional golfer from the United States who has competed on the PGA Tour and the Champions Tour. McCumber enrolled at the University of Florida and played for the University Men’s Team after graduating from high school. He is 32 years old.

Professional Life

Tyler began playing golf with his father at a young age. He was a member of the High School golf team, as well as baseball, surfing, and BMX riding. Likewise, he was a member of the University of Florida golf team before turning professional in 2014, shortly after graduation.

He made his PGA Tour Latinoamerica debut in 2015 after winning the Florida Open. At the Ecuador Open, he won his second title. McCumber won three tournaments on the PGA Tour Canada in 2018, including the Osprey Valley Open, Syncrude Oil Country Championship, and The Players Cup. He has yet to win a PGA Tour title.
