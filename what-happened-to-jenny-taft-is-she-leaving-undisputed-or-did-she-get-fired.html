What Happened To Jenny Taft? Is She Leaving Undisputed Or Did She Get Fired?

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Jenny Taft recently got into hot water with the Disputed’s co-host, Skip Bayless. After that, fans are concerned about her being fired or leaving the show.

Jenny Taft is a renowned sports reporter, host, and journalist. She is best known for featuring on the sports debate show titled Undisputed with Skip and Shannon.

Taft studied broadcast journalism at Boston University. She began her career with Fox Sports North as an on-air host and sports reporter followed by in-arena host for Minnesota Twins and Wild.

In 2013, Jenny was hired full-time by Fox Sports. With them, she reported the 2015 FIFA Women’s World Cup US. Next, she served as a news anchor and correspondent for FOX Sports 1.

In addition, Taft is a sideline reporter for college football and a pit reporter for Monster Energy Supercross, and MotoGP. Further, she is a host for Undisputed since 2019.

Did Jenny Taft Leave Undisputed Or Was She Fired?

Sports reporter, Jenny Taft has not left the show Undisputed and she is not fired as well.

She had a heating argument with Skip Bayless on the show a few weeks earlier. After that, her fans were worried that the argument could get her fired.

But that was not the actual case since both Taft and Skip had valid points and were sharing their POVs.

However, for some time, Jenny might not appear on the show. She is a sideline reporter and it is college football season. So, she has a lot of grounds to cover.

Jenny Taft Husband: Who Is She Married To?

Jenny Taft is living a wonderful life with her handsome husband, Matt Gilroy.

He is a popular and pro ice hockey defenseman. He currently represents  SC Rapperswil-Jona Lakers in the National League (NL).

Previously, Matt played for clubs like New York Rangers, Tampa Bay Lightning, Ottawa Senators, and Florida Panthers. Further, he was an NCCA player with Boston University.

It is obvious that Taft and Gilroy first met at Boston University. They gradually fell in love and exchanged wedding vows in 2015.
