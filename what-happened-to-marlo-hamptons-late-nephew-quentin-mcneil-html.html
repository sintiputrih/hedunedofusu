What happened to Marlo Hamptons late nephew Quentin McNeil?

Publish date: 2024-05-13

Marlo Hampton, a star of The Real Housewives of Atlanta (RHOA), faced a devastating loss in 2020 when her beloved nephew, Quentin McNeil, met a tragic end. In Season 15, Episode 3, Marlo shared the heart-wrenching news, leaving fans curious about the details surrounding this sorrowful event.

The Fateful Day: August 12, 2020

On that fateful day, tragedy struck as Quentin McNeil, affectionately known as “Memphis Mack,” lost his life. A fatal gunshot in southeast Atlanta claimed the life of this vibrant soul. The Atlanta Journal-Constitution reported the arrest of Fashon Raquan Ramsey-Waldron, 23, who now faces charges of felony murder.

According to the Atlanta Police, a physical altercation preceded the tragic incident. The dispute took a grievous turn, culminating in the fatal shooting. Quentin, at just 33 years old, was taken from this world in a home where disagreement turned to devastation.

Remembering Quentin: The Life of the Party

Marlo fondly recalls Quentin as the “life of the party.” She acknowledges the occasional clashes, a testament to their candid natures. Quentin’s authenticity endeared him to many. His infectious spirit lit up any gathering.

In the months leading up to his untimely passing, Quentin contributed his energy and talents to Kandi Burruss’ establishment, the Old Lady Game restaurant. Marlo expressed a desire for Kandi to acknowledge Quentin’s dedication, reaching out in the wake of her nephew’s tragic demise.

A Painful Reminder: Marlo’s RHOA Revelation

In 2023, Marlo opened up to her RHOA co-star, Drew Sidora, sharing the immense pain of losing her blood nephew. The raw emotions spilled over, leaving Marlo and Drew in a poignant exchange. Marlo’s anguished plea for acknowledgment resonates with anyone who has known the depths of grief.

Kandi Burruss, in a heartfelt response, extended her condolences, offering solace in the face of unimaginable loss.

The passing of Quentin McNeil is a testament to the enduring pain that Marlo Hampton carries. His memory lives on through her, a reminder of the fragility of life and the strength it takes to forge ahead after such a heartrending loss.
