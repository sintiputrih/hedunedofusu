Who is Jesse James (TV personality) 3rd wife Sandra Bullock?

Publish date: 2024-05-02

Jesse James is a 63-year-old American entrepreneur and television personality born in Long Beach, California.

In his TV series Jesse James: Monster Garage, Jesse Gregory James was the focus of a show that documented some of the custom motorbike projects. Before being canceled, the program aired on the Discovery Channel in 2012.

Who is Jesse James (TV personality) 3rd wife Sandra Bullock?

James got married to superstar actress Sandra Bullock in 2005. They met on the set of James’ show Monster when her godson who was an avid fan of the show went for a tour on the set.

The marriage did not last long, however. They finally got divorced in 2010 citing irreparable differences as the reason.

Between 2010 and 2014, Sandra Bullock was the highest-paid actress in the world. In 2010, she was ranked among the world’s 100 most influential people by Time magazine.

For her work in The Blind Side, she received a Golden Globe and an Academy Award.
