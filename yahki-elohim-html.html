Yahki Rapha Elohim - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-14

Herbalist, reiki healer, dietitian, musician, and spiritual holistic teacher who founded the organization known as The Risen Black Messiah Alliance. His videos cover topics like herbology, biochemistry, and ancient mystical principles.

Before Fame

As an adolescent, he associated with gangs and drugs. He was incarcerated in the year 2009. He eventually chose his present path as he didn't want to return to his life of crime, nor did he want to hurt others or spend anymore time in jail.

Trivia

He is a proponent of plant-based recipes. He has also promotes conscious dieting, activism, agriculture, food programs, and knowledge of self. He has amassed more than 240,000 subscribers on his Yahki Awakened YouTube channel.

Family Life

He was born Alexander Hickman. He was primarily raised by his maternal grandmother. In the year 1994, he moved to St. Louis with his mother and siblings. 

Associated With

He and Asia Suler are both known for sharing content about natural medicine.
