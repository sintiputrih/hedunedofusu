Zooey Deschanel Goes Without Makeup in Morning Selfie: Picture

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Who's that girl? It's not Jess! Zooey Deschanel took to Twitter on Saturday, May 17 to share a makeup-free bathroom selfie, looking uncharacteristically subdued.

The New Girl actress, 34, who plays upbeat school teacher Jessica Day on the hit Fox comedy, posed with a sleepy expression, rumpled hair, and a loose shirt that read "French Kissing in the U.S.A." 

"Good morning! #nomakeup #nofilter," she captioned the picture.

The casual morning look was a drastic departure from Deschanel's sleek Met Gala attire on May 5. The actress, who is currently designing her own collaboration with Tommy Hilfiger, wore a classic navy Hilfiger gown to the fashion-forward event, with her hair down and curled. 

"She doesn’t take herself too seriously," Hilfiger said of Deschanel to Entertainment Weekly. "She lives her life with a sense of humor. Certainly [anyone can think of] a lot of Hollywood actors and Hollywood types who take themselves very seriously — and certainly in the fashion business, people take themselves very seriously — but we don’t."
